Cornell Daily Sun

From 2016-2018, I wrote for the Arts and Entertainment section of the Cornell Daily Sun. In 2018, I was also one of the editors for that section. All of my articles were published online, and some in print. I wrote music, book and film reviews, as well as about local artists in the Ithaca area. Some articles were a co-written with other members of the staff.
