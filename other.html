Sound Design

As part of a sound design class that I took, I scored and/or added sounds to bits of existing films using a sound library and Pro Tools. I scored the opening scene of Contact with a cover of "Neptune" by Sufjan Stevens and edited sounds. I also added sounds to the classic car chase scene in Ronin, but added Charli XCX's fun, modern song "Vroom Vroom." I created an original score for the short film Triangulation, and edited sound effects in as well. All these projects can be found in the following link:
