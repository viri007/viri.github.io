About Me

Who I'm, What I do

I grew up in a small town in south Texas until 2016, when I moved to Ithaca, NY for 4 years to study Information Science at Cornell University. During my time at Cornell, I feel like I tried many new things, and don't regrete any of that at all. I became an editor of the arts section of the school paper, president of coffee club, and vice president of Watermargin Cooperative.

My time at Cornell would've been much harder for me had it not been for my pet parrot BMO, who I brought to Ithaca in 2018 and now continues to live with me back in Texas. I enjoy bird-watching, needle felting, embroidering, cooking, playing piano, and learning about insects in my free time.
